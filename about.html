The History

PVZ restaurant is an award winning restaurant. It was first opened in 2000 by Mrs. Emma Rose. The first branch was in New York.
After over 20 years, we have become one of the best restaurants in the country with over 10 branches around the country.

MEET EMMA ROSE

EMMA ROSE, founder of PVZ restaurant, was born on January 25, 1985. She got into cooking after she watched her mom cooking and founded PVZ restaurant at the age of 25 years old.
Soon, PVZ restaurant became popular due to her cooking skills and was awarded countless times.
